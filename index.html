Our Story

As they continued to speak, the trainee suddenly passed by and my eyes were immediately drawn to her. I was stunned. I think it was love at first sight. Since then, I'd purposely pass by the production area just to catch a glimpse of her, leaving sticky notes on her tumbler, as I am too shy to speak to her. But fate had a way of bringing us together.

We became friends, grew closer and found comfort in each other. That's when I started to court her for months on end. While I was performing as a lead guitarist in our company's silver anniversary, my eyes were all on her. Only her. Her captivated eyes shone brightly in that sea of crowd from where I was standing. On that day, I finally got the answer I was waiting for.

Looking back, I can't help but smile as I remember how our story began. It was an unexpected encounter that led to a love that I never knew existed. From strangers, to friends, and eventually lovers. Our relationship went through many obstacles, but we overcame them all because of our love. And now, as we celebrate our wedding day, I can't help but feel grateful for the journey that brought us here. To my dear wife-to-be, you are the light that brightens up my life and I promise to love and cherish you for all eternity. I am blessed to have found you, and I can't wait to spend the rest of my life with you. Here's to forever, my love.
